About Me

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor in Arizona and in Indiana. I have been in the mental health field for 13 years with experience in community mental health and private practice. I am EMDR level 1 and 2 trained and find a deep sense of appreciation for trauma work. I am from Arizona where I have lived a majority of my life and moved to the North East part of Indiana a few years ago. I provide telehealth only services to people living in Arizona or Indiana.

I have had a passion for the mental health field for the majority of my life. My own lived experiences have created a foundation of empathy and interest to heal in my soul that has led me to wanting to be a support for others. I love to see growth in people and it is genuinely at the heart of what I do.

Outside of work, you can find me out in nature- near water specifically, listening to an audio book and reading a physical book, cheering on my Arizona Wildcats or spending time with my family.
